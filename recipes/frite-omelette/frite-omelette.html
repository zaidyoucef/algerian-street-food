Frite Omelette: Or French Fries Omelette

Don't want to cook, empty fridge, don't panic, this little recipe will save you!

Total:15 mins

Prep:5 mins

Cooking:10 mins

Serving:2

    Ingredients

  • 3 Medium sized potatoes
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt and pepper

    Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes, clean them and cut them into slightly thick fries.
  2. Heat a large quantity of olive oil in a saut√© pan, dip the fries in it and let them brown for about ten minutes. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Beat the eggs into an omelet with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour a drizzle of olive oil in a frying pan, add the omelette and the fries. Bake.
