Kebda Mchermla

Total:50 mins

Prep:10 mins

Cooking:40 mins

Serving:4

    Ingredients

  • 500g Lamb liver
  • 3 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp Karwiya caraway
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Â½ tbsp vinegar
  • Oil

    Steps

  1. First prepare the dersa:
  2. Crush the garlic with the spices (caraway, paprika, salt and pepper).
  3. Fry the dersa in a little oil.
  4. Fry the whole liver in a tablespoon of oil on both sides.
  5. Take it out and cut it into small pieces.
  6. Arrange the pieces of liver in the dersa.
  7. Leave to cook over low heat.
  8. Add water if necessary.
  9. When cooked, drizzle with vinegar and remove from heat.
  10. Serve hot with good homemade bread.
