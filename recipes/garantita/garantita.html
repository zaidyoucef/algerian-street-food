Garantita

Total:40 mins

Prep:10 mins

Cooking:30 mins

Serving:4

    Ingredients

  • 200g Chickpea flour
  • 1 Liter of water
  • Salt and cumin
  • 1 Egg
  • Â¼ Liter of milk
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 1 Tea glass of oil
  • 2 Portions of cheese

    Steps

  1. Mix the chickpea flour and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add the water and mix everything.
  3. In another bowl, add the egg, whisk well.
  4. Pour into the chickpea and water mixture.
  5. Add the mustard and the oil + (2 portions of Laughing Cow Cheese) and mix well (you can mix in a blender).
  6. Cover and let stand 1 hour.
  7. In the meantime preheat the oven to 200 C and place a mold (pyrex for me) to heat it.
  8. Oil the mold and pour the preparation.
  9. Add 2 tbsp of oil and mix with a fork.
  10. Bake for 20 minutes (30 minutes for me, it all depends on the oven) in a convection oven (if you don't have a convection oven at the end of cooking, turn on the grill).
  11. Remove from oven, sprinkle with cumin.
  12. Serve with good bread and harissa
